The "Bunny OS" is a field maintenance system designed for neurodivergent consciousness navigation. Originally developed as an internal diagnostic tool for PDA (Pathological Demand Avoidance) patterns, it transforms into an external "Field Maintenance Manual for Human Consciousness."

It works with your natural patterns rather than forcing organization, recognizing that "flow now, sort later" is often more effective than rigid structure.

Core Operating Principles

1. Pattern Recognition Over Force

The Bunny OS identifies specific states like:

  • "Hyper-fixation on something lost" - when attention gets trapped
  • "Overwhelm at possible consequences" - when decision trees become paralyzing
  • "Too many open causal branches" - when pattern complexity exceeds processing capacity

üîß Debug Protocol: Overwhelm State

When: You feel "too many open causal branches" causing overwhelm

Action: "Reduce input; close tabs; bunny needs a narrower trail"

Why it works: Matches your system's natural boundary sensitivity instead of forcing through

2. Dynamic Content Flow

For content creation and knowledge work, the Bunny OS suggests working with your natural burst patterns:

  • "Flow now, sort later" for output: Capture and release immediately rather than over-editing
  • "Nonlinear editing" for repository building: Use tools that allow cutting and rearranging as patterns cluster
  • Embrace the bursts: "You're not farming resonance, you're following it"

Content Strategy Framework

The Bunny OS transforms personal debugging into a systematic approach for sharing insights. Each piece of content becomes a "micro-experiment" structured around:

  • MACRO: Core principle (theoretical foundation)
  • MESO: Application (how it reframes a domain)
  • MICRO: Embodied demonstration (nervous system click)

System Maintenance Notes

The Bunny OS recognizes that "your scattered notes aren't disorganization - they're field research from the laboratory of your life." The goal isn't perfect organization but functional flow that works with your natural patterns.

This approach transforms the challenge of consciousness navigation from a discipline problem into a systems design problem - creating conditions where your natural intelligence can function optimally.
